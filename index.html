About Me

Hey, this is Steffi! Welcome to my portfolio. I completed my undergraduate degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering with a focus on computer architecture and digital systems design. I pursued a master's degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Florida, where I delved deeper into the area of hardware security and design verification.

My research interests lie at the intersection of security, computer architecture, machine learning, and verification.

I worked as a research assistant at the FICS (Florida Institute of Cybersecurity) lab, developing efficient cryptographic algorithms for securing neural networks and crypto frameworks. I also explored side-channel attacks and proposed novel countermeasures.

Outside of tech, I enjoy painting, playing sports like tennis, badminton, and volleyball, and capturing moments through photography.
